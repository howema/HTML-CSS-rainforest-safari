About Green Rainforests

Green Rainforest Biodiversity

Rainforests are teeming with life. They are known as the coral reefs of the land with how many various species live within their depths. The defining feature of these ecosystems is the closed and continuous tree canopy, which fosters the moisture-dependent vegetation, which is like, really cozy.

Sadly, many rainforests around the world have been heavily subjected to, and are in danger of, deforestation.
